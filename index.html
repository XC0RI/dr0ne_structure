DR0NE

Dr0ne is a practice formed by Kian Matine and Philipp Burwinkel in 2025, working across architecture, Kunst am Bau and set design. The work draws from a self-built universe of references across subcultures — driven by an interest in the sublime, violence, and absurdity. Active across Switzerland, with experience from offices in Zurich and Munich.
